What is a Manufacturing Record Book?

A Manufacturing Record Book (MRB), also called MDR Quality Dossier, is a structured compilation of all manufacturing, inspection, and testing records required by clients and third-party inspectors.

Why it matters:

  • Client rejection prevention
  • FAT clearance acceleration
  • Payment hold elimination
  • Project close-out success
