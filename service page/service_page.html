What Is a Manufacturing Record Book?

A Manufacturing Record Book (MRB), also called MDR or Quality Dossier, is a structured compilation of all manufacturing, inspection, and testing records required by clients and third-party inspectors.

An incomplete MRB can lead to client rejection, FAT clearance delays, payment holds, and project close-out issues.

We ensure this does not happen to your project.

Our MRB Service Scope

1. Complete MRB Compilation

  • MRB cover page and document index
  • Approved Inspection & Test Plan (ITP)
  • Material traceability and MTC verification
  • Welding and NDT documentation
  • Dimensional, pressure, and functional test records
  • Painting / coating records
  • Final inspection release note

4. Final Submission Support

  • Clear indexing for client review
